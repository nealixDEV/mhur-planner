Ability Cards (Match Pickups)

Ability Cards provide temporary buffs during a match. Card Level (Lv.1–Lv.9) only increases the buff duration—the strength of the buff remains the same.

Strike Card
• Effect: +10% Attack Power.

Assault Card
• Effect: 20% Damage Reduction.

Rapid Card
• Effect: +10% Movement Speed, +10% Attack/Animation Speed, and enables Wall Running.

Technical Card
• Effect: +50% Skill Reload Speed.

Support Card
• Effect: Creates a healing field that restores 50 HP over 5 seconds to yourself and nearby allies.

Ability Manifest (Tamaki Amajiki Special Tuning)

Ability Manifest increases the duration of all Ability Card buffs. It does not increase the potency of the buffs themselves—only their active duration.

Duration Multiplier by Level
Lv.1: ×1.10
Lv.2: ×1.15
Lv.3: ×1.20
Lv.4: ×1.25
Lv.5: ×1.30
Lv.6: ×1.35
Lv.7: ×1.40
Lv.8: ×1.45
Lv.9: ×1.50
Lv.10: ×1.55
Lv.11: ×1.60

Formula
Final Duration = Base Card Duration × Ability Manifest Multiplier

Example (Lv.9 Ability Card)
Base Duration: 53s

Manifest Lv.1 → 58.3s
Manifest Lv.5 → 68.9s
Manifest Lv.9 → 79.5s
Manifest Lv.11 → 84.8s

Notes
• Ability Manifest affects Strike, Assault, Rapid, Technical, and Support Cards.
• It only extends duration and never increases attack, defense, speed, healing, or reload values.
• Multiple Ability Cards of the same type stack their effects normally; Ability Manifest only modifies how long those effects remain active.
